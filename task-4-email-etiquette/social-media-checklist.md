# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. **Do curate a professional image:** Share academic achievements, projects, and positive milestones.
2. **Do adjust your privacy settings:** Ensure personal accounts are private and you know exactly who can see your content.
3. **Do think before you post:** Assume everything you post is permanent and can be seen by future employers.
4. **Do engage positively:** Network with peers and professionals respectfully.
5. **Do verify information:** Check the credibility of news or articles before sharing them to avoid spreading misinformation.

## Don'ts
1. **Don't post illegal or inappropriate content:** Avoid anything involving underage drinking, illicit substances, or discriminatory language.
2. **Don't vent about professors or peers:** Keep grievances offline; digital complaints can easily backfire.
3. **Don't overshare personal details:** Avoid posting your real-time location, ID cards, or financial information.
4. **Don't engage in cyberbullying:** Never participate in online harassment, even as a "joke."
5. **Don't ignore spelling and grammar:** Even on social media, poorly written public posts can reflect badly on your professionalism.
